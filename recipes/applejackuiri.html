Apple Jackuiri Recipe

Description

A simple but potent mix of Jack Daniels Apple, lime juice and triple sec

Ingredients

  • 45ml Jack Daniels Apple
  • 30ml Fresh Lime Juice
  • 30ml Triple Sec
  • Sliced apple for garnish

Steps

  1. Add all Ingredients to a cocktail shaker
  2. Fill with ice and shake until ice forms on the outside of the shaker
  3. Strain into a martini glass and garnish with a slice of apple
