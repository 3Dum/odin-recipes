Cannellini Bean Recipe

Description

A beautiful little Jamie Oliver recipe and a favorite of mine. Beautiful.

Packed with protein, this is a seriously tasty side for fish, chicken or even just on toast

Ingredients

  • 1x 300g can cannellini beans
  • Any veggies you have in the fridge (Mushroom, courgette, carrot all work well)
  • 1 lemon
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • Half teaspoon chilli flakes
  • Chopped fresh rosemary and thyme

Steps

  1. Heat some olive oil on a medium heat in a large non-stick pan
  2. Add chopped veggies of your choice and cook until starting to soften
  3. Add 2 chopped garlic gloves, chilli flakes and the chopped fresh herbs
  4. Cook for two minutes
  5. Add the can of beans with all their juices
  6. Season and simmer for a few minutes until thickened
  7. Add lemon juice to taste
  8. Serve!
