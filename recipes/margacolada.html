Marga-Colada Recipe

Description

A Lukey original cocktail. Margarita meets Pina Colada. Tequila, coconut, lime, pineapple - a tropical sensation!

Ingredients

  • 30ml Coconut 1800 Resporado Tequila
  • 30ml Triple Sec
  • 30ml Pineapple Juice
  • 30ml Fresh Lime Juice
  • Lime slice to garnish

Steps

  1. Add all Ingredients to a cocktail shaker
  2. Fill with ice and shake until ice forms on the outside of the shaker
  3. Strain into a stemless wine glass and garnish with a slice of lime
